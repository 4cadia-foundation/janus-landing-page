What is Janus?

The Janus Project believes in the usability, security, control and privacy of individual data. Our mission is to facilitate the transition, use Web 3.0 applications spreading monetization in the model of multiple profit centers that share value in an open network and find a new forms and perspectives of applications developed to revolutionize the market, totally decentralized in Web 3.0.

Features

  • Indexer

    Make your own content available in the Web 3.0. Help us make internet great again.

  • Search

    Find content in the Web 3.0. Discover a new world of possibilities.

  • P2P Ads

    Opt-in to receive cryptocurrency for your attention. Only from advertisers you choose.

How it Works

  • Indexer

    Allows users to upload content to a decentralized storage, such as IPFS, Storj or Swarm. Once the content is uploaded, it will be indexed by Janus Smart Contract, currently implemented in Ethereum Rinkeby testnet. Users can choose any storage node or contract address, guaranteeing the decentralization of the Janus Platform.

  • Search

    Discover what the Web 3.0 has to offer. Leveraging user-curation , you get much more precise search results and can earn cryptocurrency to assess the quality of results for your subjects of expertise. Furthermore you will not be tracked and then stalked with annoying ads.

  • P2P Ads

    Advertisers wll be able to pay users for their attention directly using cryptocurrency. Users willl be free to choose when and what kind of ads they want to see. No more multi-billionary companies earning tons of money at the expense of users and advertisers.
